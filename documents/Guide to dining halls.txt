Livi is amazing. There’s a great/decent cook to order specialty monday-Thursday. There’s also a pasta line where they make it in front of you, a hibachi grill style part, and a burger bar. Commonly regarded as the best dining hall

Busch is mid, the pasta line is really good, but the rest misses most days. During my 2 years on Busch I used to just go to Livi to eat most days, even if my suitemates would stay on Busch or I had already come back from classes (most of my classes were on Livi)

Neilson on c/d idk much about but it’s definitely not terrible

Brower Commons on College Ave isn’t going to reopen till the renovation/rebuild is complete so I’ll just skip it. Just hope they keep wall of cereal and the well above average pizza intact🥹

Neilson and Busch have takeout. Brower is supposed to also have takeout next year since the actual dining hall is not open. Livi takeout has been closed since March 2020 (it didn’t reopen even after remote learning ceased)
